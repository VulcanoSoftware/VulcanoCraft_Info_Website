About VulcanoCraft

A professional Minecraft server network that grew out of a shared hobby in 2022.

The Owners

VulcanoCraft and Hestou are the founders and owners of the VulcanoCraft Network. Their shared Minecraft journey started around 2020 with small private servers for friends. During this period they experimented a lot with temporary servers, including Aternos servers, to gain experience and learn.

VulcanoCraft

Founder and owner of the VulcanoCraft Network.

  • Performance optimisation and custom features

Hestou

Founder and owner of the VulcanoCraft Network.

  • Community management and staff coordination
  • Design, organisation and player experience

Our Vision & Values

VulcanoCraft offers optional paid content to support the network, but is strictly against pay-to-win. There is no staff abuse, no unfair advantages and a zero tolerance policy towards cheating.

Future Vision

VulcanoCraft is a long-term project with the ambition to exist for at least 10 years. By listening to feedback and prioritising quality over fast growth, we want to remain a stable and trusted place for players.
