Gamemodes

VulcanoCraft offers several gamemodes so there is something for everyone.

Survival

The main server of the network, focused on long-term survival projects.

  • Land claims and protection
  • Economy with shops and trading
  • Events and seasonal content

Oneblock

Start on a single block and work your way up to a full island.

  • Phases with new blocks and mobs
  • Co-op possible with friends
  • Challenges for extra rewards

Bedwars

Fast PvP minigame where you have to destroy your opponentsâ€™ beds.

  • Team and solo queues
  • Item shop and upgrades per island
  • Short, action-packed matches

VulcanoTechCraft

Technical survival with mods such as automation and machines.

  • Focus on factories and automation
  • Compatible with the VulcanoCraft ecosystem
  • Ideal for players who love technology

Klokrise

Exclusive server for Poggers with unique rules and events.

  • Only accessible for selected players
  • Special seasons and storylines
  • More focus on community and roleplay

Lobby

The central hub of the network where you can choose all gamemodes.

  • Information about all servers
  • Cosmetics and small minigames
  • Quick portals to every gamemode

Velocity

The proxy layer that connects all servers.

  • Seamless server switching
  • Better performance and scalability
  • Increased stability of the network
